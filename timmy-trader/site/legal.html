Legal desk

Legal.

Plain-language policies for a trading tool that can touch real money. This is not legal advice.

Risk disclosure

Timmy is software for scanning, sizing, paper logging, and guarded order routing. It does not guarantee profits, prevent losses, or replace your responsibility for your brokerage account.

Automated tools can rely on assumptions that may be wrong for current market conditions. Live orders can be rejected, delayed, partially filled, filled at worse prices, or lose money.

Terms of use

Use Timmy only where you are legally allowed to trade and only with accounts you own or are authorized to operate. You are responsible for broker permissions, account funding, trading settings, taxes, and compliance with market rules.

Timmy is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or guaranteed by any broker named in setup materials. Broker names are used only to explain compatible workflows.

Privacy policy

This static website does not ask for broker credentials. The contact form opens your local email app and does not save messages on this site. Timmy credentials are entered in the desktop app and should remain on the user's machine.

Do not send account IDs, access tokens, passwords, app secrets, or private trading records through public support channels.

Security notes

Verify checksums before install. Keep broker keys out of Git, screenshots, and chats. Revoke broker access if you suspect credentials were shared or exposed.

Accessibility

The public pages use semantic headings, keyboard-friendly links, visible labels, and responsive layout targets so casual users can get to download, support, and legal pages without extra friction.
