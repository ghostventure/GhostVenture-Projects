Straight answers

FAQ.

Short version: Timmy is an assistant, not a money printer. It can automate work, but the account owner owns the risk.

Does Timmy guarantee profit?

No. It scans, sizes, records, and routes according to controls. Markets can still move against a trade.

Can it trade fractional shares?

Yes, when the broker and symbol support fractional trading and Timmy's fractional settings are enabled.

Does it need Webull Desktop open?

No. Webull Desktop is just a GUI. Timmy works through broker/API configuration when those credentials and permissions are valid.

Where do credentials go?

Inside Timmy's Setup tab. The site does not collect broker keys, secrets, usernames, passwords, or account IDs.

What does paper mode do?

Paper mode lets Timmy scout and record simulated decisions so the trading logic can be observed before live money is involved.

Who maintains the site?
