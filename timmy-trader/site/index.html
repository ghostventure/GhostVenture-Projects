What Timmy handles

Timmy keeps the trading desk focused on movement, liquidity, sizing, and risk instead of making users babysit every scan.

Find active tickers

It scans market lists, promotes names with enough movement, and keeps quiet tickers out of the trading lane.

Size before it swings

Fractional shares are preferred when a full share is too large or buying power needs a cleaner fit.

Keep receipts

Paper and live events feed back into the desk so the next pass can see what is working, rejected, or below expectations.

Built for casual finance users

Plain settings, clear account checks, and ready-made profiles keep setup closer to a desk workflow and farther from a terminal chore.

Setup tab first

Credentials stay local. Timmy verifies broker readiness before saving the profile used for trading.

Live is guarded

Live trading depends on account permission, buying power, market rules, and Timmy's own limits. No public page promises returns.
